Certainly! Here's a basic recipe for white sauce pasta:


Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of pasta (such as penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Chopped parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

    • Instructions:

    1. Cook the Pasta:

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  • Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

    • 2. Prepare the White Sauce:

  • In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  • Once the butter has melted, add the flour to the saucepan.
  • Stir continuously with a whisk until the flour is well combined with the butter and turns a light golden color, about 1-2 minutes. This mixture is called a roux.
  • Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  • Cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 5-7 minutes.
  • Season the sauce with salt, black pepper, and nutmeg to taste. Nutmeg adds a subtle flavor, but you can omit it if you prefer.
  • If using Parmesan cheese, stir it into the sauce until melted and well combined. This will add richness and depth to the sauce.

    • 3. Combine the Pasta and Sauce:

  • Add the cooked pasta to the white sauce in the saucepan.
  • Gently toss the pasta until it is evenly coated with the sauce.
  • Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes over low heat, stirring occasionally, to allow the pasta to absorb some of the sauce and flavors.

    • 4.Serve:

  • Once the pasta is heated through and well coated with the sauce, remove the saucepan from the heat.
  • Serve the white sauce pasta hot, garnished with chopped parsley or basil if desired.
  • You can also sprinkle some extra Parmesan cheese on top before serving for an added cheesy touch.

    • Enjoy your creamy and delicious white sauce pasta! Feel free to customize it by adding your favorite vegetables, cooked chicken, or seafood to make it even more flavorful.
