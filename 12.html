Sizzle



here's a simple recipe for a delicious chicken burger:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Condiments of your choice (mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, etc.)

    • Instructions:

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, egg, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  • Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty.
  • Heat a grill or skillet over medium heat. If using a skillet, you may want to add a little oil to prevent sticking.
  • Once the grill or skillet is hot, place the chicken patties on it. Cook for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until the patties are cooked through and golden brown on the outside.
  • While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  • Assemble the burgers by placing a chicken patty on the bottom half of each bun. Top with lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, sliced onions, and your choice of condiments.
  • Place the top half of the bun on top of the toppings and serve immediately.

    • Enjoy your homemade chicken burgers! Feel free to customize them with your favorite toppings and condiments.
