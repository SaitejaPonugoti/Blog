Here's a simple recipe for making Maggi noodles:

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • Maggi masala seasoning (included in the packet)
  • Vegetables (optional, such as chopped onions, carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (optional)

    • Instructions:

  • Heat water in a saucepan until it comes to a boil.
  • Once the water is boiling, add the Maggi noodles to the saucepan.
  • Let the noodles cook for about 2 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  • If you're adding vegetables, you can add them now. Let them cook along with the noodles for another 1-2 minutes.
  • Once the noodles are cooked and the vegetables are tender, reduce the heat to low.
  • Add the Maggi masala seasoning packet to the saucepan. Stir well to combine the seasoning with the noodles and vegetables.
  • If you prefer, you can also add a tablespoon of cooking oil at this stage for added flavor and to prevent sticking.
  • Let the noodles simmer for another minute or so until everything is well combined and heated through.
  • Turn off the heat and transfer the cooked Maggi noodles to a serving bowl.
  • Your Maggi noodles are now ready to be enjoyed! You can garnish them with some chopped fresh coriander leaves or grated cheese if you like.

    • Feel free to adjust the seasoning and add your favorite ingredients to customize the dish to your taste!
