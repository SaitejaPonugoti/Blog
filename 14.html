Here's a simple recipe for Veg Pulao:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Basmati rice (washed and soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower, etc.), chopped
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2-3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • Lemon wedges for serving

    • Instructions:

  • Heat ghee or oil in a heavy-bottomed pan or pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and bay leaf. Sauté until aromatic, about 1-2 minutes.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another 1-2 minutes until the raw aroma disappears.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  • Add chopped vegetables and mix them with the masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pan. Mix gently with the vegetables.
  • Add water or vegetable broth and give everything a good stir.
  • If using a pressure cooker, close the lid and cook on medium heat for 2 whistles. If using a pan, cover it with a lid and cook on low heat until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
  • Once done, remove from heat and let it sit covered for another 5 minutes.
  • Fluff the pulao gently with a fork. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro leaves.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side.

    • Enjoy your flavorful and aromatic Veg Pulao!
