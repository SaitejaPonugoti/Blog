Sizzle



Mix curry is a versatile dish that can include a variety of ingredients depending on personal preferences and what you have on hand. Here's a basic recipe to get you started:

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup chickpeas (canned or cooked)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions:

  • Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
  • Add the diced onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Add the minced garlic and ginger, and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Stir in the diced bell pepper, carrots, potato, cauliflower florets, and green beans. Cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  • Add the chickpeas to the pot and stir to combine.
  • In a small bowl, mix together the curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Add this spice mixture to the pot and stir well to coat the vegetables and chickpeas.
  • Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  • Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning with salt as needed.
  • Serve the mix curry hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves. Enjoy it as is or with rice, naan, or any other side of your choice.

    • Feel free to customize this mix curry recipe by adding your favorite vegetables, proteins like tofu or chicken, or adjusting the spices to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy your delicious mix curry!
