Here's a recipe for a delicious mutton biryani:


Ingredients:

the mutton marinade:

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

    • For the rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 4-5 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste

    • For assembling:

  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup fried onions (optional)
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

    • Instructions:

  • Marinate the mutton pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Allow it to marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator for best results.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add the thinly sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set them aside for garnishing.
  • In the same pan, add the marinated mutton along with the marinade. Cook on medium heat until the mutton is tender and the masala is thick. This might take around 30-40 minutes. Keep stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
  • While the mutton is cooking, prepare the rice. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the soaked basmati rice, bay leaf, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and salt. Cook until the rice is 70-80% cooked. Drain the rice and set aside.
  • Once the mutton is cooked, layer the rice over it evenly. Sprinkle chopped mint leaves, coriander leaves, and fried onions on top of the rice. Drizzle saffron-infused milk over the rice for a beautiful aroma and color.
  • Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is completely cooked and fluffy.
  • Once done, gently fluff up the biryani with a fork. Serve hot with raita, salad, and your favorite pickle.

    • Enjoy your flavorful mutton biryani!
