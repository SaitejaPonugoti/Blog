Sizzle



Making a paneer pizza is a delicious way to combine Indian flavors with the beloved Italian dish. Here's a simple recipe for you:


Ingredients:

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water

    • For the Toppings:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

    • Instructions:

    1. Prepare the Dough:

  • In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the olive oil and the yeast mixture.
  • Mix until a dough forms, then knead it on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

    • 2. Prepare the Toppings:

  • Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the paneer cubes and sauté them until they turn golden brown on all sides. Remove from heat and set aside.
  • Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  • Arrange the sautéed paneer cubes, sliced onion, bell pepper, and tomato on top of the cheese.
  • Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  • Season with salt, pepper, red chili flakes (if using), and dried oregano.

    • 3. Bake the Pizza:

  • Place the pizza on a baking tray or pizza stone and bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  • Once done, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves, if desired, before slicing and serving.

    • Enjoy your delicious homemade paneer pizza!
