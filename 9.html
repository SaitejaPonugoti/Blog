Sizzle



Here's a simple recipe for homemade chocolate ice cream:


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: chocolate chips, chopped nuts, or chocolate syrup for toppings

    • Instructions:

  • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt until well combined.
  • In a separate bowl, combine the heavy cream, milk, and vanilla extract. Slowly add this mixture to the cocoa powder mixture, whisking continuously until smooth and well combined.
  • Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  • If desired, stir in any additional toppings like chocolate chips or chopped nuts during the last few minutes of churning.
  • Transfer the ice cream to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  • Once frozen, scoop the chocolate ice cream into bowls or cones and serve with your favorite toppings such as chocolate syrup or whipped cream.

    • Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate ice cream!
