Mutton Briyni

Mutton Biryani is one of my favorite dishes and definitely one of the most
requested meals at my house. This one-pot meal is made of fragrant basmati rice,
tender spiced Mutton, and caramelized onions, and it can be cooked in less than
an hour while maintaining the same authentic flavor and texture of traditional
stovetop versions. Paired with cooling raita and a squeeze of fresh lime makes for a
hearty and delicious meal.
